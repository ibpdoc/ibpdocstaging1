Enhancements

Following is the enhancement with this release:

Third-Party Library Updates

This release is also bundled with a significant enhancement to our third-party library which includes several upgraded libraries, including PostgreSQL, hsqldb, commons-net, commons-fileupload, REST v2.0, jQuery, and Bootstrap. These updates bring numerous benefits in terms of reliability, scalability, and performance. Furthermore, we have addressed any known issues by incorporating bug fixes and security patches for these third-party libraries. This upgrade is designed to provide our users with a more secure and stable environment, ultimately enhancing their overall experience.
